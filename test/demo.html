With our racquet demo program you can try various racquets before you decide which one is best for you. There is a one time fee of $40 at the start of the program, which allows you to try 1-2 racquets at a time per week. The program last for 6 weeks, during which we will dilligently monitor your comments regarding each racquet. Finally once you decide, we will apply the $40 fee to the purchase price of your racquet.
